About Me

Hello! i'm Muhamad

I know how it feels to be unemployed with no clear path forward.

In 2017, I was totally lost. After being laid off with no savings, I felt like getting a new job was next to impossible.

What did I do to get from where I was in 2017 to where I am today doing a job I love, a job that gives me flexibility, allows me to pay my bills, and allows me to plan for the future? I learned technical skills.

It wasn’t easy, and I didn’t do it overnight, but that decision to gain clear, marketable, technical skill changed everything for me. And I know it can change everything for you too.
